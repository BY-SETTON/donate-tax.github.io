Donate to Charity and Claim Tax Benefits

Support meaningful causes and enjoy tax deductions by donating to registered charities. Follow the steps below to make a difference and benefit from SARS tax deductions.

Steps to Claim Your Tax Deduction

  1. Choose a registered NGO. You can view the list of qualified NGOs here.
  2. Make your donation and ensure you receive a Section 18A tax certificate from the NGO.
  3. Log into your SARS eFiling account and navigate to the "Tax Returns" section.
  4. Submit your Section 18A certificate under the "Deductions" section for charitable donations.
  5. Keep your certificate for your records in case SARS requests verification.

It's that simple! Start donating and make a positive impact today.
