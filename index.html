Donate and Claim Tax Benefits

You can choose where your tax goes

Follow these simple steps to make a difference you believe in.

  1. 1
    Choose a Charity

    Choose a registered charity eligible for tax deductions. View the list of qualified NGOs.

  2. 2
    Make Your Donation

    Make a donation to the charity and request a Section 18A tax certificate. This certificate is required to claim your tax benefits.

  3. 3
    Log In to SARS eFiling

    Log in to SARS eFiling and navigate to the "Tax Returns" section to submit your ITR12 return.

  4. 4
    Attach the Certificate

    Attach the Section 18A tax certificate to your tax return as proof of your donation.

  5. 5
    Submit Your Return

    Submit your return and monitor your SARS account for updates on your tax claim.
